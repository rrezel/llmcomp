Towers of Annoy is a two person towers of hanoi with n (n<25) towers and m (m<10) disks (n>m)
The hero tries to move m disks from t_1 to t_n using t_i intermediate disks
The villain triest to thwart the hero by moving the last disk the hero moved to an adjacent tower. The villain also cannot place a larger disk on top of a smaller disk.
If the hero is unable to complete the game in 2^m+1 rounds, the villain wins.
This will be a roundrobin tournament similart to blobbytictactoe with parellel games.
